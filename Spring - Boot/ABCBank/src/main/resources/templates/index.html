    • About our Bank

      A bank is a financial institution that accepts deposits from the public and creates a demand deposit while simultaneously making loans. The word bank was taken into Middle English from Middle French banque, from Old Italian banca, meaning "table", from Old High German banc, bank "bench, counter". Benches were used as makeshift desks or exchange counters during the Renaissance by Florentine bankers, who used to make their transactions atop desks covered by green tablecloths.
